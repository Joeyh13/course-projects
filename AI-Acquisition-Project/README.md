# AI-Acquisition Project

## Overview
This project contains financial analysis data on major technology acquisitions in the software and semiconductor industries. The dataset includes companies acquired in recent years with detailed financial metrics.

## Files
- `tech_acquisitions_with_formulas.csv` - Financial data for 50+ technology acquisitions

## Data Columns
- **Company**: Name of acquired company
- **Purchase Price**: Acquisition price in USD
- **Revenue (t)**: Revenue at time of acquisition
- **Cost of Revenue**: Cost associated with revenue generation
- **Operating Income (t)**: Operating income at time of acquisition
- **Total Assets**: Total assets of the company
- **Total Debt**: Total debt outstanding
- **Total Equity**: Total shareholder equity
- **Total Cash**: Cash and equivalents
- **Current Liabilities**: Short-term liabilities
- **Financial Leverage (FL)**: Total Assets / Total Equity ratio
- **Debt-to-Equity (D/E)**: Total Debt / Total Equity ratio
- **Cash Ratio**: Cash / Current Liabilities
- **Contribution Margin Ratio (CMR)**: (Revenue - Cost of Revenue) / Revenue
- **Operating Leverage Effect (OLE)**: Measure of operational risk

## Key Acquisitions Included
- Microsoft: LinkedIn, Slack Technologies, Tableau Software, GitHub, and more
- Broadcom: Qualcomm, Avago Technologies, and semiconductor companies
- Intel & Qualcomm: Wireless and chip technology acquisitions
- Cloud platform acquisitions (Salesforce, ServiceNow)
- Enterprise software deals
